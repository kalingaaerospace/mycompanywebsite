Magnetic Propulsion System

Our core innovation is a magnetic propulsion system, enabling efficient, fuel-less movement for satellites and spacecraft.

Prototype Details

We use ferrite cores and copper coils, controlled by an ESP microcontroller and MOSFETs, to generate and manage magnetic thrust.

Solar Power Integration

Our system integrates Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) to provide sustainable energy for propulsion and onboard systems.
