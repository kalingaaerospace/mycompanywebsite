About Us

At Kalinga Aerospace, we revolutionize space access by offering affordable rocket launches, efficient satellite development, and comprehensive payload integration and testing.

We Are Kalinga Aerospace

Company Overview:
Kalinga Aerospace is at the forefront of the aerospace industry, dedicated to making space access more affordable and efficient. Founded in 2024 by Sujeet Samal, our mission is to transform the landscape of space exploration through innovative technology and cost-effective solutions.

Vision:

Cost Reduction: We aim to reduce the cost of launching rockets from the current $2000 per kilogram to under $100, making space access as economical as purchasing a mobile phone in India.
Innovation and Excellence: We strive to be leaders in aerospace technology, continuously innovating and improving our solutions to meet the needs of the future one in India.
