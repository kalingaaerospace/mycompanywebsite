K-SAT Satellite

We provide cost-effective rocket launch services, reducing the cost per kilogram to space significantly. Our goal is to make space accessible to a wider audience by bringing down the price to under $100 per kilogram.

The K-SAT Satellite is a planned CubeSat designed for small-scale experiments in space. It has the following dimensions:

  • Height: 10 cm
  • Width: 10 cm

This compact satellite is equipped with a camera capable of taking photos and uses a LoRa module for communication. The K-SAT can be used for a variety of purposes, including prototyping and conducting small-scale scientific experiments.

The cost of making the K-SAT Satellite is estimated to be around $200-$400 (approximately ₹15,000-₹30,000), and its weight will be under 1500 grams.

Uses of CubeSats

CubeSats like the K-SAT Satellite offer several advantages and uses:

  • Scientific Research: CubeSats can be used for scientific experiments in space, including climate research, space weather monitoring, and biological studies.
  • Technology Demonstration: They provide a platform for testing and demonstrating new space technologies at a lower cost.
  • Educational Purposes: Universities and educational institutions use CubeSats to provide hands-on experience to students in space missions and satellite design.
  • Commercial Applications: CubeSats can be used for commercial purposes such as Earth observation, communication services, and asset tracking.
  • Prototyping: CubeSats serve as a cost-effective way to prototype and validate new space technologies and mission concepts.

Overall, CubeSats like the K-SAT Satellite are revolutionizing access to space by providing a cost-effective and versatile platform for a wide range of applications.
